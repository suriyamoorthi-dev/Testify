📊 Expected & Previous Year Cutoff Marks

Welcome to Testifyy's detailed analysis of exam cutoff marks for various competitive government exams in India. Cutoff marks are the minimum scores required to qualify for the next stage of the selection process. While they can vary each year based on exam difficulty, the number of vacancies, and the number of candidates, understanding previous year cutoffs is a powerful way to set realistic target scores for your preparation.

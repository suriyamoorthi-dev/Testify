📚 Formula Sheet for Competitive Exams

🧮 Percentage

  • Percentage = (Value / Total) × 100
  • Increase/Decrease % = (Difference / Original) × 100
  • Percentage point change = New Percentage - Old Percentage

🚶 Speed, Time, Distance

  • Speed = Distance ÷ Time
  • Time = Distance ÷ Speed
  • Distance = Speed × Time
  • Average Speed = Total Distance / Total Time
  • Convert km/hr to m/s: Multiply by (5/18)
  • Convert m/s to km/hr: Multiply by (18/5)

💰 Simple Interest

  • SI = (P × R × T) ÷ 100
  • Amount (A) = P + SI
  • P = Principal, R = Rate (per annum), T = Time in years

💱 Compound Interest

  • Amount (A) = P × (1 + R/100)T
  • CI = A - P = P × [(1 + R/100)T - 1]
  • When interest is compounded Half-yearly: Rate = R/2, Time = 2T
  • When interest is compounded Quarterly: Rate = R/4, Time = 4T

📐 Area & Volume

  • Area of Square = side²
  • Perimeter of Square = 4 × side
  • Area of Rectangle = length × width
  • Perimeter of Rectangle = 2 × (length + width)
  • Area of Circle = πr²
  • Circumference of Circle = 2πr or πd
  • Area of Triangle = ½ × base × height
  • Volume of Cube = side³
  • Surface Area of Cube = 6 × side²
  • Volume of Cuboid = length × width × height
  • Volume of Cylinder = πr²h
  • Curved Surface Area of Cylinder = 2πrh
  • Total Surface Area of Cylinder = 2πr(h + r)

📊 Average

  • Average = Sum of observations ÷ Number of observations
  • Weighted Average = (w₁x₁ + w₂x₂ + ...) / (w₁ + w₂ + ...)

📈 Profit & Loss

  • Profit = Selling Price (SP) - Cost Price (CP)
  • Loss = Cost Price (CP) - Selling Price (SP)
  • Profit % = (Profit / CP) × 100
  • Loss % = (Loss / CP) × 100
  • SP = CP × (100 + Profit%) / 100
  • SP = CP × (100 - Loss%) / 100
  • Discount = Marked Price - Selling Price
  • Discount % = (Discount / Marked Price) × 100

👷 Time & Work

  • If A does a work in 'n' days, A's 1-day work = 1/n
  • (A + B)'s 1-day work = 1/n + 1/m (if A takes n days, B takes m days)
  • Work = Men × Days (M₁D₁ = M₂D₂)
  • Work = Men × Days × Hours (M₁D₁H₁ = M₂D₂H₂)

🔢 Ratio & Proportion

  • Ratio a:b = a/b
  • Product of Means = Product of Extremes (a:b :: c:d ⇒ bc = ad)
  • Compounded Ratio of (a:b) and (c:d) is (ac:bd)
  • Duplicate Ratio of a:b is a²:b²
  • Sub-duplicate Ratio of a:b is √a:√b

🧮 HCF & LCM

  • Product of two numbers = HCF × LCM
  • HCF of Fractions = HCF of Numerators / LCM of Denominators
  • LCM of Fractions = LCM of Numerators / HCF of Denominators
